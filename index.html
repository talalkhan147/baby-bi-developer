Services

Services We Offer,

Data sources and Connectivity

First step in creating a Power BI solution is connecting to the data sources. This can be Spreadsheets, SQL databases, SharePoint sites or any other source. It is important to understand how to connect and extract data from sources.

Data Modeling

This involves creating relationships between tables, creating calculated columns and measures, and transforming and shaping the data to meet your needs.

Visualization

Power BI is all about creating compelling visualizations that help users understand data. Need to understand how to create a variety of visualizations, including charts, tables, matrices, maps, and more.

DAX

DAX is the language used by Power BI to create calculations and queries. It is a powerful language that allows you to perform advanced analysis and calculations on your data.

Power Query

Power Query is a data transformation and cleansing tool that is built into Power BI. It allows you to extract, transform, and load data from a variety of sources. It's important how to use Power Query to transform your data before modeling it.

Power BI Service

The Power BI Service is the cloud-based platform where you publish and share your Power BI reports and dashboards. Important to understand how to publish reports to the Power BI Service, how to configure security and permissions, and to create and manage content for sharing with others.
